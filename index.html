Biography

Last updated on 27th Oct, 2020

I’m currently a Pre-doctoral Researcher (Brain Resident) in Earth Observation Science Lab of Google Research India, mentored by Dr. Varun Gulshan. I am working on self-supervised representation learning for satellite imagery.

Prior to that, I was an intern in Real Virtual Humans lab at Max-Planck-Institut für Informatik under the guidance of Gerard Pons-Moll. Our work TailorNet was accepted for ORAL presentation at CVPR 2020.

I graduated with Institute Gold Medal in May 2019 with B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad. There, I worked under Dr Avinash Sharma at CVIT for my honours project.
